Custom Services

We offer a range of custom services to suit all of your industrial needs.

Hose

Hydraulic, Air brake, Water Hose and Gauges, Push-Connect, Brewery Hose, Fire Hose, Suction Hose, Chemical Hose, Ducting, Hose Clamps, and yes, even Garden Hose.

Tubing

Including PTFE, PVC, Nylon, Silicone and whatever else you may need.

Rubber, Gaskets & Seals

We have many types and sizes of rubber, Klinger, and other gasket-making materials available. Need a custom gasket cut or made? No problem! We do it all the time.
